# What is Clicks

Clicks is a city-wide partner meetup series for the Shopify ecosystem.

Same format, every city. 2 hours, hard out.

## The idea

The Shopify ecosystem is full of agencies, app developers, and operators who don't have a regular reason to be in the same room. Clicks creates that reason.

No panels, no keynotes, no sponsor pitches. Just speed rounds and real conversation.

## The format

- 40–80 people
- 3–4 speed round speakers (5 min each)
- One co-host partner
- 2 hours. Hard out.

## The community

Clicks is volunteer-run. No staff, no budget, no central office. Each city runs itself using the same playbook.

The community lives on [Discord](https://discord.gg/beRe7aD7Tw) and is documented here on GitHub.
