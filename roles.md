# Roles

Clicks has no formal staff. Everything runs on volunteers.

## Host

Runs a specific city event. Responsible for venue, co-host, speakers, and post-mortem. One-time or recurring.

## Co-host partner

A Shopify ecosystem partner who co-runs an event with the host. Helps promote and covers some logistics. Does not control the agenda.

## Mod

Maintains the GitHub org and Discord. Reviews content PRs, manages access, keeps things running. Appointed by existing mods.

## Contributor

Anyone who submits a PR — post-mortems, content, handbook improvements, issue templates. No approval needed to contribute.

## Community member

Anyone in the Discord. No requirements.
